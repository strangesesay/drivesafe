Accident Prevention Tips

Vehicle Maintenance

Regularly check your tires, brakes, lights, and fluid levels to ensure your vehicle is in optimal condition.

Avoid Distractions

Put your phone away, avoid eating while driving, and focus solely on the road ahead.

Speed Management

Always obey speed limits and adjust your speed according to road and weather conditions.

Rest Well

Take breaks every 2 hours on long trips and never drive when you're feeling drowsy.

Weather Awareness

Adjust your driving for rain, fog, or other adverse weather conditions by reducing speed and increasing following distance.

Seatbelt Safety

Always wear your seatbelt and ensure all passengers are properly restrained before driving.
